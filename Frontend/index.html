// 01 — Features

Built for Cross-Chain DeFi

ChainLend leverages LayerZero's omnichain messaging infrastructure to enable seamless lending and borrowing across distinct blockchain networks.

⛓️

Cross-Chain Messaging

Borrow requests travel from Sepolia to Amoy via LayerZero, locking collateral and releasing loans without any manual bridging.

🔒

Collateral Locking

Your mUSDC deposits on Amoy are automatically locked as collateral when you initiate a borrow. 50% LTV ensures protocol safety.

📈

Earn While You Wait

Deposited collateral earns 5% APY interest on Amoy. Withdraw your principal plus accrued interest after repaying your loan.

🪙

Stablecoin Loans

// 02 — Protocol Flow

How It Works

Four simple steps to borrow across chains. The protocol handles all cross-chain messaging and collateral management automatically.

01

Deposit Collateral

Deposit mUSDC into the lending pool on Polygon Amoy. Your deposit immediately starts earning 5% APY interest.

→ Polygon Amoy
02

Request Borrow

Initiate a borrow request on Ethereum Sepolia. A LayerZero message is sent to Amoy to lock your collateral at 50% LTV.

→ Sepolia → LayerZero → Amoy
03

Receive Loan

Once collateral is confirmed locked on Amoy, sUSDC stablecoins are released to your wallet on Sepolia.

→ Ethereum Sepolia
04

Repay & Withdraw

Repay principal + 10 sUSDC flat fee on Sepolia. LayerZero unlocks your collateral on Amoy, then withdraw your mUSDC + earned interest.

→ Sepolia → LayerZero → Amoy

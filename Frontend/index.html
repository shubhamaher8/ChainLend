Complete User Journey
1
Switch to Polygon Amoy → Deposit mUSDC

Connect MetaMask, switch to Amoy, mint test tokens via the Faucet tab, then deposit mUSDC into the LendingPool. Your funds start earning 5% APY immediately.

2
Switch to Ethereum Sepolia → Borrow sUSDC

Switch network to Sepolia. Enter a borrow amount (max 50% of your Amoy deposit). Quote the LayerZero fee, then submit. A cross-chain message locks your Amoy collateral.

3
Wait ~1-2 Minutes

LayerZero relays the message to Amoy, locks your collateral, sends confirmation back to Sepolia, and sUSDC is released to your wallet.

4
Use Your sUSDC Freely

Your borrowed sUSDC is in your Sepolia wallet. Interest accrues at 8% APR. Your Amoy deposit continues earning 5% APY in the background.

5
Repay Debt → Unlock Collateral

On Sepolia, repay the full debt (principal + interest). A LayerZero message signals Amoy to unlock your collateral.

6
Switch Back to Amoy → Withdraw

Your collateral is now unlocked. Withdraw it along with the 5% APY interest you earned while it was deposited.
